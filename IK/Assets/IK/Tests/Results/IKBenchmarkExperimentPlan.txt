IK Benchmark Experiment Plan

Goal
Compare CCD, Jacobian Transpose, fixed-damping DLS, and SVD-adaptive DLS under a one-dimensional step-scale sweep.

Fixed Solve Settings
maxIterations=500, positionTolerance=0.1, warmupSamplesPerSolver=1, totalReach=3.2

Sweep Axis
step_scale
stepScaleSweep=0.0001, 0.0002, 0.0005, 0.001, 0.002, 0.005, 0.01, 0.02, 0.05

Sample Design
Reachable: 200 samples, ratio range [0.15, 0.95]
Unreachable: 200 samples, ratio range [1.05, 1.35]
Samples are fixed by random seed 20260406 and reused for every solver and every step scale.

Rotation Deviation Metric
Average per-joint local-rotation offset from the initial pose, measured in degrees at solve end.

Solver Series
CCD: family=CCD, step_scale=1
JacobianTranspose: family=JacobianTranspose, step_scale=1
JacobianDLS_lambda_0.03: family=JacobianDLS, damping=0.03
JacobianDLS_lambda_0.10: family=JacobianDLS, damping=0.1
JacobianDLS_lambda_0.30: family=JacobianDLS, damping=0.3
JacobianSvdDLS_default: family=JacobianSvdDLS, minimum_damping=0.02
